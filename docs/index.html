Iran's response to Israeli Operation Rising Lion strikes on Iranian nuclear facilities. Also known as the "Twelve-Day War." Waves targeted Israeli military bases, critical infrastructure (Haifa refinery, power stations), and IDF command centers. Houthi forces coordinated with proxy attacks.

  • Bat Yam residential strike: 9 killed, 200+ injured
  • Bazan Oil Refinery Haifa: 3 killed, refinery shut down
  • Weizmann Institute Rehovot: $500M–$1B estimated damage
  • Glilot intelligence base: direct missile hit (satellite confirmed)

Dramatically expanded scope vs. TP3 — targeting US military facilities across the Persian Gulf (Bahrain, Qatar, Kuwait, UAE, Oman), coalition naval vessels, and Israeli targets simultaneously. Launched in response to US-Israeli Operation Epic Fury / Roaring Lion strikes on Iran.

  • USN Fifth Fleet HQ Bahrain targeted in Wave 1
  • Port Jebel Ali, UAE and Duqm port, Oman struck by drones
  • Tel Aviv impacts: 1 fatality, 21 injuries in Wave 1
  • Multi-layered coalition response: US, UK, France, Jordan, UAE
Notable TP3 Impacts (Twelve-Day War)
  • Tamra residential building: 4 Arab Israelis killed
  • Petah Tikva 20-story building: 4 killed (including Holocaust survivor)
  • Bnei Brak: 1 killed
  • Rishon LeZion: 2 killed, 17 injured
  • Ramat Gan residential: 1 killed, 9 buildings destroyed
  • Beersheba final wave (Jun 24): 4 killed, 20 injured
  • Haifa Power Plant: 2 missiles, damage and fire
  • Soroka Medical Center Beersheba: struck, 80 injured
  • Kirya IDF HQ Tel Aviv: ballistic missile damage confirmed
  • Tel Nof airbase: missile hit confirmed by satellite
  • Eshkol Power Station Ashdod: struck, power outages
  • US Embassy Branch Office Tel Aviv: shockwave damage
